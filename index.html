Quiz Application Rules:
1. The quiz application consists of a total of 20 questions. Among these questions, 4 are difficult, 8 are medium, and 8 are easy level questions.
2. Each quiz session will contain 5 randomly selected questions. These questions will consist of 1 difficult, 2 medium, and 2 easy questions.
3. Questions will be scored based on their difficulty levels. Difficult questions are worth 30 points, medium questions are worth 20 points, and easy questions are worth 15 points.
4. You have 2 eliminate one option lifelines in the quiz application. You can use this lifeline to eliminate 3 incorrect options out of the 4 provided.
5. Additionally, you have 2 hint lifelines in the quiz application. You can use this lifeline to get a hint for any question you desire.
6. Points will be awarded for each correct answer. There will be no deduction or awarding of points for incorrect answers.
7. The user's performance will be evaluated based on the accumulated points at the end of the quiz.
8. There is no time limit to complete the quiz. You can enter your answers within your desired time frame.
9. Only one answer option can be selected for each question. Selecting multiple answer options will be considered invalid.
