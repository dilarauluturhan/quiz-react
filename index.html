Quiz Application Rules:
1. Your quiz will consist of a total of 20 questions: 4 difficult level, 8 medium level, and 8 easy level questions.
2. Each user will be presented with 5 randomly selected questions. Out of these, 1 will be difficult level, 2 will be medium level, and 2 will be easy level questions.
3. If you correctly answer a difficult level question, you will receive 30 points.
4. If you correctly answer a medium level question, you will receive 20 points.
5. If you correctly answer an easy level question, you will receive 15 points.
6. Each question will have 4 options, and you need to select the correct option.
7. You will have a chance to eliminate an answer twice.
8. You will have 2 hints. When you request a hint, you will receive a clue that will help you better understand the question.
9. You will earn points for each correct answer, and incorrect answers will not affect your score.
10. In the end, the scores of the correctly answered questions will be totaled, and your overall score will be displayed.
